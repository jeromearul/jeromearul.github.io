ABOUT >> we are a Providence RI based cnc-routing shop. we offer custom fabrication and prototyping services for artists, makers, small businesses, creatives, inventors, students, and educators. we use precision computer-numerically-controlled (cnc) routers to cut, mill, engrave, and carve. we can cut most hardwoods, softwoods, plywoods, plastics, brass and aluminum. we can also route 4'x8' plywood boards. we can make signs, jigs, fixtures, molds, components for furniture, and architectural projects.
